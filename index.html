What's inside

The real pitfalls
families face

Most families don't realize what can go wrong until it already has. This guide walks you through the six most common — and costly — mistakes so you can avoid them entirely.

01
Emotional attachment clouding judgment
Sentimental value is real — but it's not market value. Learn to separate the two before listing.
02
Mispricing the property
Pricing isn't guesswork — it's strategy, data, and discipline. Overpricing costs you time and money.
03
Sibling assumptions & misalignment
Assuming everyone agrees without a clear process is how families fall apart during the sale.
04
Neglecting repairs & presentation
Buyers don't see memories — they see deferred maintenance. Address issues proactively.
05
Inadequate marketing strategy
A yard sign is not a strategy. Today's buyers find homes online — you need to meet them there.
06
Ignoring legal & financial details
Estate rules, tax implications, and disclosure requirements need professional guidance early.
